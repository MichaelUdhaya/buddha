Eligibility for Admission

For apply to +1, student must be passed in S.S.L.C. / C.B.S.E.
We not showing any partialty for other school students. Good mark will consider as first preference.

Candidates should have completed the age of 15 while apply.

Admission forms

Application forms are available in school office room.
The application form must be filled in the Candidate’s own handwriting and submitted on the next day itself.

Carry these things along with you
  • A Passport size photograph.
  • Xerox copy of Ration card, Aadhar card, Birth Certificate received from Elementary School/ Corporation/ Municipality/ Panchayat Union / Panchayat Board office, should be enclosed and originals should be produced for verification.
  • A pen for filling the forms.
  • Original and Xerox copy of Community Certificate obtained from the Competent Authority must be produced to get the Application Form if belonging to SC/ST/MBC/DNC or BC.
  • TC and other original certificates
